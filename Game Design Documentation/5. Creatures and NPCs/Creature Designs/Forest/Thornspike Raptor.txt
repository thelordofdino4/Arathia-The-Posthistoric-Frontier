Aggressive and Territorial: The Thornspike Raptor is known for its aggressive nature, particularly when defending its territory or hunting.

Gliding: Capable of gliding short distances (4-6 times its body length) using its wing arms, aiding in ambushes and quick escapes.

Territorial Zones: Establishes and defends territories that provide ample prey and cover.

Enhanced Agility and Strength: Over generations, the Thornspike Raptor's musculature and skeletal structure became optimized for bursts of speed, powerful jumps, and agile maneuvers. Behavioral Evolution:

Pack Hunting: Thornspike Raptors began to exhibit pack hunting behaviors, coordinating attacks to take down larger prey. This social structure not only increased their hunting success but also provided protection against larger predators.
Territoriality: The Thornspike Raptor's aggressive nature and powerful defenses made them highly territorial. They established and defended territories with vigor, using their spiked tails and agility to ward off intruders.
Modern Thornspike Raptors:

Current Adaptations: Today, the Thornspike Raptor is a highly specialized predator perfectly adapted to the dense forests of Arathia.
